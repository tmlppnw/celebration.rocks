About The Celebration

The Celebration is a social event, bringing together people from all over North America and the world! There will be live music, food, booze, and loads of fun! The event is open to the public, and all are welcome.

This is a FREE event!

Who we are is the possibility of being fun, expressed, and loving! And we are causing the experience of ourselves as a connected community, exploding with aliveness and possiblity. Come be a part of it!
